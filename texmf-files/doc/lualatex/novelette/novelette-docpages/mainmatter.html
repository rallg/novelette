What is Main Matter?

Main Matter, or mainmatter is a standard publishing term. It is "the story itself" from start to finish.

Mainmatter pages show headers and/or footers, according to chosen layout. Chapter openings, and sometimes other special pages, do not print the header and may not print the footer.
